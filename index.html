Big Cats

Big cats are powerful predators known for their strength, speed, and beauty. They include lions, tigers, leopards, and jaguars. These animals are apex predators and play an important role in keeping ecosystems balanced.

The Goat

Goats are intelligent, curious, and playful animals. They are known for their climbing abilities and their unique rectangular pupils. Goats have been domesticated for thousands of years and provide milk, meat, and companionship.

Sharks

Sharks are ancient ocean predators that have existed for over 400 million years. They come in many sizes, from tiny dwarf sharks to massive whale sharks. Despite their scary reputation, most sharks are harmless to humans and are essential for healthy marine ecosystems.
